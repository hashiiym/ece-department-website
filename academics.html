Academic Programme

Academics

The department offers a B.Tech programme in Electronics and Communication Engineering affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University. The academic experience balances strong fundamentals, laboratory practice, design thinking, and professional growth.

Programme Snapshot

  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • KTU affiliated curriculum
  • Project-based learning and lab integration

Core Learning Areas

  • Electronic circuits and network theory
  • Signals, systems, and communication engineering
  • Microprocessors, embedded systems, and IoT
  • Control systems, DSP, and VLSI foundations

Laboratory Exposure

  • Analog and digital electronics labs
  • Communication and signal processing practice
  • Microcontroller and embedded application work
  • Project labs and seminar reviews

Student Development

  • Mini projects and final-year capstone work
  • Technical events and certification support
  • Presentation, teamwork, and professional communication
  • Industry and higher-study readiness
